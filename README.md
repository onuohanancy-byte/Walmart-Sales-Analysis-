## Walmart Store Sales Performance & Holiday Impact
## Project Overview
This project analyzes Walmart weekly sales performance and the impact of holidays on revenue across 45 stores.

## Tools Used
-Power BI
- SQL
Excel
Correlation Findings
Holiday weeks generally generated higher sales than regular weeks.
Store performance varied significantly across locations.
Sales trends showed noticeable spikes during holiday periods.
DAX Measures
Holiday Sales Uplift % = DIVIDE( [Holiday Avg] - [Non-Holiday Avg], [Non-Holiday Avg] )

## Dashboard Features
Total Sales KPI
Number of Stores KPI
Average Weekly Sales KPI
Holiday Sales Uplift %
Weekly Sales Trend
Top 10 Stores by Revenue
Bottom 10 Stores by Revenue
Sales Distribution by Store
